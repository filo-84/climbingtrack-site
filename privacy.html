Privacy Policy

ClimbingTrack respects your privacy and processes personal data only to provide the functionality of the application.

Data collected

  • Email address (for account identification)
  • User profile information (optional username)
  • Climbing activity data (ticks, ratings, comments)

Purpose of processing

The collected data is used solely to provide the functionality of the ClimbingTrack app such as tracking climbed routes, displaying statistics and managing user accounts.

Data sharing

No personal data is sold or shared with third parties except when required for technical operation of the service.

User rights

Users may request deletion of their account and personal data at any time via the app or by contacting support.
