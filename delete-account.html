Delete your account

Users can permanently delete their account directly in the ClimbingTrack app under:

Settings → Delete Account

Alternative request

If you cannot access the app anymore, you can request account deletion via email:

support@deinemail.de

Data that will be deleted

  • Account data (email address, user ID)
  • Saved climbs / ticks
  • Ratings and comments

Data retention

Data is removed from the active database immediately. Backup data may remain for up to 30 days for technical reasons and will be deleted automatically afterwards.
